How to Reset Your Password

If you forgot your password, follow these steps:

Step 1: Go to the login page at https://support.example.com/login.
Step 2: Click the "Forgot Password?" link below the password field.
Step 3: Enter your registered email address and click "Send Reset Link."
Step 4: Check your email inbox (and spam/junk folder) for a message from support@example.com.
Step 5: Click the reset link in the email. Note: This link expires after 24 hours.
Step 6: Enter your new password. It must be at least 8 characters long and contain one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.
Step 7: Click "Confirm" and log in with your new password.

Troubleshooting:
- If the reset link has expired, repeat the process from Step 1 to generate a new link.
- If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support at help@example.com.
- If your account is locked after multiple failed attempts, wait 30 minutes before trying again or contact our support team directly.
- For security, password reset links can only be used once. If you click the link and it says "invalid," request a new one.

Contact: For further assistance, email help@example.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST).
