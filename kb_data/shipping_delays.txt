Shipping, Delivery, and Tracking Information

Standard Shipping:
- Delivery takes 3-5 business days from the date of shipment.
- Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $50.
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on business days are typically shipped the same day.

Express Shipping:
- Delivery takes 1-2 business days.
- Express shipping costs a flat rate of $9.99 per order.
- Available for domestic orders only.

International Shipping:
- Delivery takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination country.
- International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees.
- Not all products are eligible for international shipping.

Tracking Your Order:
- Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link.
- You can also track your order by logging in to your account and clicking "Track Order" under "Order History."
- If tracking shows "Delivered" but you have not received the package, please wait 24 hours as carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered before the final drop-off.

Shipping Delays:
- During peak shopping seasons (Black Friday, holidays), shipments may experience a delay of 1-3 additional business days.
- Severe weather conditions or carrier disruptions may also cause unexpected delays.
- If your order has not arrived within the expected delivery window, please contact our support team.

Lost or Damaged Packages:
- If your package is lost in transit, contact us within 15 days of the expected delivery date for a replacement or refund.
- If your package arrived damaged, take photos of the damage and contact us within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement at no extra cost.

Contact: For shipping inquiries, email shipping@example.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.
