Refund and Return Policy

Our Refund Policy:
Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date. After 30 days, refunds will not be accepted.

Eligibility:
- The item must be in its original, unopened packaging and in unused condition.
- Digital products, gift cards, and subscription services are non-refundable.
- Items purchased during clearance or final sale events are non-refundable.

How to Request a Refund:
Step 1: Log in to your account and go to "Order History."
Step 2: Click the order you want to return and select "Request Refund."
Step 3: Choose a reason for your return from the dropdown menu.
Step 4: Print the prepaid shipping label that will be emailed to you.
Step 5: Ship the item back to us using the label within 7 business days.

Processing Time:
- Once we receive the returned item, our team will inspect it within 2-3 business days.
- Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- You will receive an email confirmation when the refund has been issued.

Partial Refunds:
- Items that show signs of use or damage may qualify for a partial refund (up to 50% of the purchase price) at our discretion.

Exchanges:
- If you would like to exchange an item instead of requesting a refund, please contact our support team at help@example.com. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Contact: For refund-related questions, email billing@example.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.
